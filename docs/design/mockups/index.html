📝 How to Review

As you explore each mockup, consider:

  • Do the colors feel right? (Too bright? Too dark?)
  • Is the spacing comfortable? (Too tight? Too loose?)
  • Are the animations smooth? (Too fast? Too slow?)
  • Is the information hierarchy clear?
  • Does it feel premium/professional?
  • What excites you most?
  • What needs improvement?

Note: These are interactive HTML mockups with simulated behavior. The real implementation will have full functionality, real audio, and backend integration.
